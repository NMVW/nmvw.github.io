Remote Living

My life lived as a nomad.

Why Remote?

Freedom of location opens up new lifestyles that are not possible when we are tied down. An office job keeps us commuting (stuck in traffic for a big piece of the day), keeps us from straying too far from the house (commute in the morning or at weekend end), and leaves little room for exploring this god given earth. Forget saving up your PTO for a measly 2 weeks traveling (not to mention the time lost in transit with flying, buses, ride shares, etc.), prepare for life on the road, you will thank yourself for the memories made later.

How Tos

Hygiene

  • Au Naturel

    Remember, you are on the road and visiting nature’s beautiful landscapes – get some biodegradable soap

  • In-Unit

    A 12-gallon water tank can get you through a nice shower assuming you have a foot pump or generator to keep the water flowing

  • Corporate

    Most gyms offer showers in their amenities, find a gym with locations that cover your transit
