Open Source

28 March 2016
People

Brilliant people are contributing to technology without expecting a return. When I first heard about a community where selfless giving was the underlying covenant, I thought of hippies living in a commune.

I justified my perception of the community by judging the comportment of a prominent figure of the open source community, Linus Torvaldus. Arguably a modern cyber hippie.

Interest

As I journey through the Hack Reactor program to deepen my abilities as an engineer, I am paralleling the growth of my faith. As a leaning idealist, I have foudn similarities between the Christian spirit and the open source manifesto. It is with this newfound sight, that I grow increasingly eager to learn about the members of such a community as the open source one.

Nick Vinson
