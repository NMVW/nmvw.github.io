Open Source

28 March 2016
Community

Brilliant people are contributing to technology without expecting a return. When I first heard about a community where selfless giving was the underlying covenant, I thought of hippies living in a commune.

My perception came from the observation of a prominent figure of the open source community, Linus Torvaldus. Arguably a modern cyber hippie.

Interest

As a software engineer challenged to handle new ideas on a frequent basis, I am paralleling the growth of my faith. As an idealist, I have found similarities between the Christian spirit and the open source manifesto. With newfound perspective, I am eager to learn about open source community and my place in it.

Nick Vinson

Nick Vinson. Software Engineer.

Boulder, CO
